This package contains 5 different tile-able road pieces suitable for "Tower Defense" games. Also comes with flat terrain pieces of 4 different sizes to place in between the road tiles.

This road tileset have 3 texture variants: a brightly lit green grass variant, a darker and more realistic grass variant, and a snow variant.

The models are in .FBX format and the textures are 512x512 .PNG’s that tile seamlessly. The package includes prefabs for all road tiles with all texture variants, so you can get started building right away.
